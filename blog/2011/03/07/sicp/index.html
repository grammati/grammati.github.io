SICP

2011-03-07

I have finally been reading SICP (after foolishly avoiding it for years), and I'm pretty blown away. I just got to section 1.3 (10% of the way into it), and it's talking about higher-order functions! This is the first chapter of a textbook for first-year undergraduates. I'm pretty sure that I know programmers with 20 years of experience who have never heard of higher-order functions.
