Lazy buggers...

2005-06-15

It drives me nuts that someone at Microsoft declared sprintf as "sprintf(const char*, const char*, ...)", because that's what I see in my intellisense every time I use it. What are the arguments? Is it format, then string, or string then format? I don't know! And because that lazy bugger at Microsoft didn't declare it as "sprintf(const char* str, const char* fmt, ...)" (or is it the other way?), I have to go and look it up every single time!
