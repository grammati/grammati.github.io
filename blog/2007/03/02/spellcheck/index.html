Spellcheck

2007-03-02

MS Word tells me that "templating" (as in "we will use Mako for templating") is not a word, and suggests "tem plating" or "tinplating" instead. Later it corrects my misspelling of "doohickies" to "doohickeys".

Hmmm.

Interestingly, Blogger's spellcheck also identifies both "templating" and "doohickies" (but not "doohickeys") as an error, but it says that there ain't no such thing as "tem" or "tinplating" either. Its suggestions are a bit odd as well - it thinks that when I typed "tinplating" above, I might have really meant "downplaying". Huh?
